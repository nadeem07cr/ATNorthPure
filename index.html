Our process
From source to container

Every feather passes through our full extraction and cleaning process before it leaves Karachi.

01
Used jacket sourcing

Pre-owned jackets purchased from secondhand clothing markets in Karachi — no animals involved.

02
Feather extraction

Jacket lining carefully cut open and down feathers extracted by hand.

03
Cleaning & sorting

Down feathers cleaned, dried and sorted by grade — white and grey separated.

04
Container packing

Packed into export containers with full China customs documentation.

100% Ethically Sourced — No Animals Harmed

ATNorthPure does not work with live animals at any stage. All our duck down and goose feathers are extracted exclusively from pre-owned jackets and clothing purchased from secondhand markets. No birds are involved in our supply chain. Our process is clean, humane, and entirely post-consumer — giving discarded materials a second life while delivering premium quality feathers for manufacturing.
